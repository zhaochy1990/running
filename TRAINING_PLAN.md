## Phase 3: Marathon Specific (Jul 20 — Sep 27, 10 weeks)

**Goal**: Build marathon-specific endurance, practice race pace, peak fitness.

**Rhythm**: High → High → Deload → High → High → Deload → High → High → Deload → Transition to taper

| Week | Type | Volume | Long Run | Session 1 | Session 2 |
|------|------|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | High | 90 km | 32 km with 12km @ 4:02/km | 6x1000m @ 3:36/km | 10km @ 3:50/km |
| 2 | High | 90 km | 30 km with 15km @ 4:02/km | 5x1000m @ 3:36/km | 10km @ 3:50/km |
| 3 | **Deload** | **75 km** | **25 km easy** | 4x1000m @ 3:40/km | 8km easy tempo |
| 4 | High | 92 km | 32 km with 15km @ 4:02/km | 3x2000m @ 3:40/km | 12km @ 3:50/km |
| 5 | High | 95 km | 35 km progressive (5:00 → 4:05/km) | 5x1000m @ 3:35/km | 12km @ 3:52/km |
| 6 | **Deload** | **78 km** | **25 km easy** | 4x1000m @ 3:38/km | 8km easy tempo |
| 7 | High | 92 km | 32 km with 15km @ 4:00/km | 4x1000m @ 3:36/km | 10km @ 3:50/km |
| 8 | High | 90 km | **30km MP checkpoint** @ 4:02/km | 3x1000m @ 3:38/km | 8km @ 3:50/km |
| 9 | **Deload** | **75 km** | 22 km easy | 3x1000m @ 3:40/km | 8km easy tempo |
| 10 | Transition | 80 km | 25 km with 10km @ 4:00/km | 3x1000m @ 3:40/km | 8km easy tempo |

**Key marathon-specific sessions**:
- Long run MP blocks capped at **15km max** to protect Achilles — build race endurance through frequency, not single-session overload
- **Day after 30+ km MP long run: complete rest or cross-train only** (no easy run)
- Practice race nutrition strategy during every long run over 30km
- Target 2-3 gels per long run, same brand/timing as race day
- Weekend long runs in the morning; if weekday quality sessions are in the evening, prioritize sleep for recovery

**Achilles management protocol**:
- Monitor dorsiflexion pain scale (0-10) on the morning after every long run
- If pain > 3/10 or persists beyond 1 day → skip next quality session, replace with easy run
- If pain recurs 2+ weeks in a row → reduce MP block by 3-5km and reassess

**Checkpoint**: 30km run at marathon pace (4:02/km) in week 8. Target: 2:00:00-2:01:00 for 30km.

## Monitoring & Adjustment Triggers

Track these weekly via `python -m coros_sync sync && python -m coros_sync analyze`:

| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Weekly km | 80-100 | 65-80 | < 65 |
| Easy run HR | < 145 | 145-152 | > 152 |
| RHR | < 50 | 50-55 | > 55 (overtraining) |
| HRV | Stable/rising | -10% drop | -20% drop (back off) |
| Z1-Z2 % | > 75% | 65-75% | < 65% (too hard) |
| VO2max | > 59 | 57-59 | < 57 (losing fitness) |
| Achilles pain (AM) | 0/10 | 1-3/10, gone in hours | > 3/10 or persists > 1 day |

**Adjustment rules**:
- 2+ red metrics in same week → cut volume 30% for 1 week
- HRV drops 20%+ → skip quality session, easy run instead
- RHR elevated 5+ bpm for 3+ days → rest day
- Illness/injury → skip Phase, don't compress. Health first.
- Achilles pain > 3/10 morning after long run → skip next quality session
- Achilles pain recurs 2+ consecutive weeks → reduce MP block length and reassess
