**Recovery week rhythm (HARD)**:
- Non-base phase ≥4w uses 3:1: 3 load weeks + 1 recovery.
- Recovery high = 70-80% of previous load week (20-30% cut), not phase lower bound.
- Next load week compares to the previous load week before recovery, not the trough. `75 -> 56-60 recovery -> 75-82` normal; `75 -> 56 -> 60` under-rebounds unless explicit fatigue/injury.
- Phase.focus states this: "3:1, W4 70-80% W3; next load resumes from W3; recovery cancels quality".
- Phase <4w may omit recovery but focus states "shorter than 4 weeks, no recovery week".
