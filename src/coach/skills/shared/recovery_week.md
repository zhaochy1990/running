**Recovery week rhythm (HARD)**:
- Any non-base phase of ≥ 4 weeks (build phase / peak phase) must use 3:1 periodization: 3 consecutive weeks of progressive load + 1 week cut down to 70-80% of that phase's lower weekly-volume bound
- The recovery-week arrangement must be stated explicitly in the corresponding phase.focus field, e.g.: "3:1 cycle, deload 1 week every 4 weeks to 70% of W3 volume; recovery week cancels all quality sessions"
- A phase shorter than 4 weeks may omit the recovery week, but focus must state "shorter than 4 weeks, no recovery week"
